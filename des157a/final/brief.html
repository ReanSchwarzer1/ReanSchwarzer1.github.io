Project Brief

Project Summary

For the final project, I am considering to significantly expand on my every picture studio with the use of more interactions and expanded visuals.

Project Goals

My specific goals right now are to make a more complex animation system for the image stack system with js, adding image scrolls when the user clicks the image, and other general quality of life improvements.

Design Strategies

I'm not planning on making any significant changes to the underlying design since I'm alreadt quite pleased with the font and color choices, as well as the overall effect. I'll see what I can do to improve font legibility, but that's all I'm planning for now.

Interaction Strategies

I've decided that these will be my main areas of focus for the project:

  • My initial thoughts were to manipulate the images and the image stacking effect I have with even more complex animations using js.
  • I would also like to have specific parts of the images clickable to pull up more details on those specific parts.
